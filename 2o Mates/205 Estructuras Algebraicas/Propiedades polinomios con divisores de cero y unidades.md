```ad-proposition

1. $\delta(pq) = \delta(p) + \delta(q) \iff r_n s_m \neq 0_R$.
2. $(r_n \in R^\times \lor s_m \in R^\times) \implies \delta(pq) = \delta(p) + \delta(q)$.
```
